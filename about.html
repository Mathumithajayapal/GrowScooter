Our Story

Founded in 2020, SparkRide began with a simple mission: to revolutionize urban transportation. What started as a small startup with just 50 scooters in downtown Los Angeles has grown into a nationwide movement with thousands of riders across 25+ cities.

Our journey has been driven by innovation, sustainability, and a passion for creating seamless mobility solutions. From our first prototype to our latest Sport Pro model, every scooter we build reflects our commitment to quality, safety, and rider satisfaction.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the electric mobility revolution, helping cities reduce traffic congestion and carbon emissions while providing an enjoyable, efficient way to get around.
